**Product Management Frameworks Repository**
============================================

A curated collection of practical, execution-first frameworks for product managers who want to ship meaningful outcomes — not just build slides.

This repository translates strategy into action. Each framework is designed to help PMs move from ambiguous ambition to clear scope, measurable impact, and real-world delivery.

**What You’ll Find**
--------------------

*   Decision-making frameworks
*   AI product strategy models
*   Metric-to-feature translation tools
*   90-day execution planning templates
*   Executive alignment structures
*   Interview and communication aids

Every framework is:

*   Outcome-driven (tied to business metrics)
*   Filter-based (forces clarity before execution)
*   Built for real constraints (time, engineering capacity, org politics)
*   Designed to ship

**Who This Is For**
-------------------

*   Product Managers (IC to Head of Product)
*   Founders building structured product thinking
*   AI product builders
*   Operators working with executive stakeholders

**Philosophy**
--------------

Most product failures don’t come from lack of ideas — they come from lack of structure between ambition and execution.

These frameworks exist to close that gap.
